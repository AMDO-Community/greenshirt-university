301.2 TASKS

301.2.1 MILPERS Manual​

A. Steps

  • Access MyNavy HR or official MILPERSMAN portal
  • Locate applicable article by topic or article number
  • Review governing policy and procedural requirements
  • Apply policy to personnel action (transfer, separation, advancement, admin action)

B. Reasons

  • Ensures personnel actions comply with Navy policy and law
  • Standardizes administration across commands

C. Control/coordination

  • Admin Officer
  • Personnel Office/CPPA
  • CMC/LCPO

D. Communications

  • Official correspondence
  • MyNavy Portal
  • Email

E. Safety precautions

  • Protect PII

F. Parameters/limits

  • Follow current instruction version only

G. Demonstration

  • Retrieve and explain correct article for a real personnel case

301.5 EMERGENCIES

Fire (Flight Deck, Line, Hangar)​

A. Indications

  • Smoke/flames/alarms

B. Immediate action

  • Sound alarm
  • Fight fire with proper agent
  • Secure aircraft/fuel

C. Operating limits

  • Maintain safe distance

D. Secondary risks

  • Explosions, toxic smoke

E. Follow-up

  • Damage assessment

F. Demonstration

  • Explain/fire drill

Crash (Flight Deck/Runway)​

A. Indications

  • Crash call

B. Immediate action

  • Emergency response
  • Rescue/firefighting

C. Limits

  • Secure area

D. Secondary risks

  • Fire/fuel

E. Follow-up

  • Mishap reporting

F. Demonstration

  • Explain response

Fuel Spill (Deck/Line/Hangar)​

A. Indications

  • Visible fuel/odor

B. Immediate action

  • Stop source
  • Secure ignition
  • Spill kit

C. Limits

  • Restricted access

D. Secondary risks

  • Fire

E. Follow-up

  • Report/cleanup

F. Demonstration

  • Spill response

HAZMAT Spill​

A. Indications

  • Leaking container

B. Immediate action

  • Isolate
  • PPE
  • Notify HAZMAT

C. Limits

  • No exposure

D. Secondary risks

  • Toxicity

E. Follow-up

  • Proper disposal

F. Demonstration

  • Explain procedures
