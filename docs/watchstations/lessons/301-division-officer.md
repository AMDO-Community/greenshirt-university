## Purpose
Lead Sailors and production through disciplined presence, standards, and problem removal.

## Key points
- Be visible, predictable, and fair.
- Set standards, then verify.
- Make it easy to do the right thing.

## Practical application
- Daily: spaces, safety, training, blockers, parts, tools.
- Weekly: review trends, QA findings, manning, top offenders.
- Monthly: qualification pipeline, audits, program health.

## Common mistakes
- Managing from email.
- Tolerating “workarounds” that hide risk.

## Oral board prompts
- How do you balance production and compliance?
- What do you do when a program is failing but the schedule is unforgiving?
